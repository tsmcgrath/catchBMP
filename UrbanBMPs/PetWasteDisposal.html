Pest Waste Disposal

What is it?

Proper pet waste disposal eliminates removes raw waste from the environment that would have otherwise been washed into wetlands or streams with storm events. It is important to to properly dispose of pet waste becuase it will contaminate water supplies with excess nutrients and potential pathogens. Pet waste can collectively contribute a considerable amount of nutrients and potential pathogens into streams.

Conservation Benefits

  • Protects surface water from extra nutrient additions
  • Protects wildlife, fish, and humans from potential pathogens

What does it include?

Proper pet disposal should include disposing of waste in trash receptacles or non-consumptive composting areas. Pet wastes should be cleaned up and removed from all public and private properites.
